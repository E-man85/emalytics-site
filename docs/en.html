About

Emalytics is a micro-consulting practice specializing in data analysis, process automation, and interactive dashboard development. We help small businesses make faster, clearer decisions through agile and tailored solutions, without the complexity of large consulting firms. Our practical experience includes projects across different business areas, focusing on turning scattered data into useful insights, automating repetitive tasks, and creating visual reports that support everyday decision-making. The goal is simple: fast, efficient, and customized solutions, regardless of the industry.
